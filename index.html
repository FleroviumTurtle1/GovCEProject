FDA too Involved in the Drug Market?

By

About

While the FDA has gotten a decent control over harmful drug quantities, they've also caused a few economic concerns related to pharmacy medicine.

There are many ways which pharmaceutical pricing and drug quantities can be affected by a simple adjustment of alloted amounts available. Multiple sources point toward the FDA causing higher prices in the drug industries. First pointing at how the government's attempt at control prescription drug pricing due to inflation may actually be causing a negative effect. "Dean Clancy, senior health policy fellow at Americans for Prosperity, told The Center Square, “Drug price controls will do nothing to reduce inflation, but will result in hundreds of fewer new cures, less hope for cancer patients, and millions of lives cut short."(“Critics Take Aim at Government Price Controls on Prescription Drugs,” 2022).

Facts

In the past the need for approval has caused a tendency of higher pricing in drug creation as seen here, "Makena, an FDA-approved medication, fulfills important unmet needs for women at high-risk. In comparison with non-FDA approved compounded drugs, Makena is manufactured in an FDA-regulated and FDA-compliant, sterile facility, with tight controls in place to ensure that every dose has the same quality and consistency--particularly important for a medication administered as a weekly injection."(Divis, 2011).

2022 Information

In the beginning of 2022 there was a request for the FDA to ease on the sperm donor rules, most of the rules related to sperm donation are in place due to pontential for disease and cost for testing them. Considering, ""The FDA believes that overlapping safeguards in place for anonymous semen donations adequately and appropriately reduce the risk of transmission of communicable diseases," an agency spokeswoman said."(Dockser Marcus, 2022). The FDA has a reason to put rules on sperm donation, though considering the request for adjustment of the FDA sperm donation ruleset it's obvious that to some extent as in most situations involving rulesets, there are rules which may need to be adjusted for some exlusions from these rules.

Counter Argument & Rebutle

While the FDA may be causing adverse effects on the medical industry it can't be ignored that most of the current rules in place often prevent the release of dangerous drugs in many situations, this can be seen in the follow piece out of an article. "The Food and Drug Administration limited the use of the Covid-19 vaccine from Johnson & Johnson after reviewing the risk of life-threatening blood clots."(Whyte, 2022). Given how they've been able to recognize this danger and put efforts into taking control of the danger it's notable that this organization can't be completely removed in any situation.

Though this doesn't entirely make up for the fact that the FDA has been running into some controversy for quite a while. To both side's benefit it's rather clear that the medical situation both economical and danger related can get quite confusing. And in most cases there may be other things which take higher priority in government budgets, "Prescription drug reform and Medicare expansion are not expected to be in the president’s upcoming budget proposal, slated to be issued this week. White House Press Secretary Jen Psaki said health policy remains a big priority"(Haberkorn, 2021).
